Steaks

All of our steaks are cooked to your favorite specifications.
All steaks include mashed potatoes and french fries.

  1. Sirloin Steak
  2. You have a choice between a 6 ounce or a 9 ounce sirloin cooked to
    perfection over a wood burning stove.
  3. T-bone Steak
  4. This slice of steak is seasoned with fine herbs and salts giving it a
    mouthwatering flavor.
  5. Porterhouse Steak
  6. This slice is slow cooked over hickory wood and slathered in fine
    homemade bbq sauce.
  7. Healy Special
  8. This dish is a house favorite. This is a compilation of all of our meats cooked to perfection
