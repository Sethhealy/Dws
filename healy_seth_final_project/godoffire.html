Steaks

All of our steaks are cooked to your favorite specifications. All steaks include mashed potatoes and french fries.

    • This slice is slow cooked over hickory wood and slathered in fine homemade bbq sauce.

    • This dish is a house favorite. This is a compilation of all of our meats cooked to perfection overnight to give a melts in your mouth texture and a sweet and savory taste with our homemade sauces and seasonings.
