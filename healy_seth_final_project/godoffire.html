Steaks

All of our steaks are cooked to your favorite specifications.
All steaks include mashed potatoes and french fries.

  • Sirloin Steak

    • You have a choice between a 6 ounce or a 9 ounce sirloin cooked to
    perfection over a wood burning stove.

  • T-bone Steak

    • This slice of steak is seasoned with fine herbs and salts giving it a
    mouthwatering flavor.

  • Porterhouse Steak

    • This slice is slow cooked over hickory wood and slathered in fine
    homemade bbq sauce.

  • Healy Special

    • This dish is a house favorite. This is a compilation of all of our meats cooked to perfection
    overnight to give a melts in your mouth texture and a sweet and savory taste with our
    homemade sauces and seasonings.

Gyros

All of our gyros made to order. All include french fries and 1 side.

  • Philly

    • Our Philly is made to perfection with a perfect amount of cheese and meats.

  • Roast beef

    • Our roast beef gyro includes banana peppers, onions and our secret sauce.

  • Chicken

    • Our chicken gyro includes lettuce, tomato and peppers.

Roasts

All of our roasts are cooked to your favorite temperature and color. Includes french fries and salad

  • Rib Roast

    • Our rib roast has been prepared over a hickory wood fire giving it a savory hickory flavor.

  • Pork Rump Roast

    • Our Pig butt is slow cooked to give a mouthwatering flavor and texture.

Ribs

Our ribs are our specialty. Includes mashed potatoes and french fries

  • Dry Rubbed Ribs

    Our dry rubbed ribs are rubbed down in the finest ingredients giving it a flavorful taste to make your mouth water and begging for more.

  • Baby Back Ribs

    • Our baby back ribs are the most tender ribs out there.
